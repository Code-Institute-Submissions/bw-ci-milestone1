A little bit about ourselves

Olympus Fitness was born from a long time dream of two fitness enthusiasts and enterprenaurs in 2019. We have started small, initially in a 100 sq ft hangar, as of today we occupy a 8000sq ft building across two floors. We don't plan on stopping here, so whether you are an invenstor looking to expand your portfolio, or a personal trainer, reach out to us today for a chat, we are always looking to grow.

Our team of well trained and experienced personal trainers is here to help you achieve your fitness goals no matter your fitness level.
